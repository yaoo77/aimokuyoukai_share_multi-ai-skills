# Sample: "How will Claude Code change our lives?"

A real comparison from AI Arena with 4 AIs + Agent Teams (5 sources total).

## Results

| Aspect | Codex (GPT-5.4) | Claude (Opus 4.6) | GLM-4.7 | Agent Team (3-person) |
|--------|-----------------|--------------------|---------|-----------------------|
| **Core message** | "Life infrastructure for turning ideas into reality" | "Democratization of programming" | "Personal secretary for all knowledge workers" | "Distance from idea to execution becomes zero" |
| **For everyday people** | Budget automation, travel planning, learning support | PTA roster management, custom budget app, community digitization | $20/month saves 60 hours = ROI of 3 salaries | Build a LINE reservation bot yourself (vs $3K outsourcing) |
| **For business** | Meeting notes, proposal drafts, data summaries | Accounting CSV conversion, marketing X post analysis | Proposals: 4-8h to 15min (97% reduction) | Sales reps build BI dashboards in minutes |
| **Structural change** | Less busywork, more thinking and deciding | "Can't do it" barrier disappears | "Solo entrepreneurship" becomes realistic | Organizations dependent on outsourcing lose their edge |
| **Risks** | (not mentioned) | Need ability to articulate "what you want" | (not mentioned) | Data destruction risk, unintended information leaks |
| **Tone** | Balanced, pragmatic | Example-rich, citizen perspective | Numbers-driven, ROI-focused | Three perspectives unified (offense/structure/defense) |

## Standout Quotes

- **Codex**: "Spend more time thinking and deciding"
- **Claude**: "From adapting to off-the-shelf products to building tools that adapt to you"
- **GLM**: "$20/month, 60 hours saved = ROI equivalent to 3 salaries"
- **Agent Team #8 (Business)**: "Think of it, try it today"
- **Agent Team #6 (Quality)**: "The cost of wrong execution also drops dramatically"
- **Agent Team #2 (Technical)**: "Requirements to deployment compressed into one sentence of natural language"

## Key Takeaway

All AIs agreed on "democratization of programming" as the core impact. The differentiation was in framing:
- **Codex**: Most concise, essence-focused
- **Claude**: Richest real-world examples (PTA, community associations)
- **GLM**: Strongest numerical persuasion (97% reduction)
- **Agent Team**: Only source that deeply explored risks alongside opportunities
